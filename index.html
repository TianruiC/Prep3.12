Seeking Interaction

I found out this America population data visualization includes almost everything about the animation and interactions as the reading mentioned.

Animation

It has an animation which changes the population chart period by period. The whole animation starts in 1950 and ends at 2060 with every 5-year changes. And you can click the Animation control which is below the chart to pause or resume the whole animation.

Interaction

Users can hover their mouse to the specific bar to view the percentange of male and female population at corresponding age group.
